Resources

Sick COVID patients across India have been forced to find ways to treat themselves at home as hospitals are choked with the surge in the number of cases and a shortage in oxygen supply during this deadly second wave of the pandemic.

We are setting up Centers where COVID positive patients - who are either asymptomatic or have mild symptoms - can isolate, away from homes that are cramped for space. This will protect their families from being infected with the virus and help stop the spread.Centers will have Oxygenated Bed that can support patients with mild oxygen requirements.

Beds With Oxygen

Each center has minimum of 20 Beds with Oxygen that can support Patients with Mild Oxygen need.

Doctors & Nurses

Care Center will have Primary Care Doctor and Nurses and Supported Remotely by specialist like Pulmonologist.

Food & Medicine

Patients will be provided 3 Meals during their stay in center
