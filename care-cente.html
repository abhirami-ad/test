Covid Care Centers

Covid Care Center : Mysuru

Address : Central Hospital (Beedi Colony), Mysore

 We have successfully launched our first COVID care center in Mysuru for underprivileged asymptomatic patients to quarantine worry-free. We are eternally grateful to our sponsors who have contributed to this cause and provide us with their continued support. Be a #COVIDWarrior yourself by sponsoring a bed for a patient a day!

Services & Facilities

Healthcare Infrastructure

Our center provides 20 beds with medication, food, investigation reports, counselling, PPE kits and facial masks free of cost to underprivileged COVID patients. They can also avail oxygen concentrators (10L x 2) and diagnostics during their stay at the center, if needed.

Medical Staff

The center has dedicated doctors, nurses and an ER specialist on standby 24/7. The patient will be given necessary attention and care from medical professionals all through their stay at the center.

Food & Medicine

Along with the beds, the center is also offering 3 meals and medication to all the patients during their stay.
